Case Study

How We Turn a Broken Store Into a Sales Machine

A breakdown of our process — and the results it produces — for a fashion brand based in Lagos.

A small fashion brand came to us with 8,000 Instagram followers, solid engagement, and zero online sales. Their bio link pointed to Instagram. People were asking "where can I buy?" in the comments — and there was nowhere to send them.

01
Discovery & Audit
We mapped their product catalog, target customer, and biggest drop-off points. Their DM-to-sale conversion rate was under 5%.
02
Store Architecture
Designed a full storefront: homepage, 6 product pages, collection view, cart, and checkout — all mobile-first since 95% of their traffic came from phones.
03
Build & Launch
Live in 10 days. WhatsApp order confirmation wired in. IG link-in-bio updated. First order came in 31 hours after launch.
04
Post-Launch Optimisation
Tweaked product descriptions and added size guides after week 1. Return visitor rate climbed to 28% by week 3.
